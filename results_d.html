You are: Dating

“Hey Alexa, play ‘Boo’d Up’ by Ella Mai.”

Wow, you’re dating! This means you’re in the early stages of a relationship. A time where you’ll find yourselves going out on dates to get to know the other person better. This is merely a prelude to being a fully fledged couple.

Remember: Communication is key! Make sure you’re both looking for the same thing. Dating is often viewed as being in “commitment” while having no real commitment with/to another person. Complicated, huh?

Think of dating as a ship. A type of ship where two people must board in order for it to work. These ships can be a very good experience or very bad. These ships more often then not sink, crash, or disappear. If you’re lucky, your ship will stay afloat for a long period of time.
